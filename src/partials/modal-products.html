Our products

What you should know about our products

  • Ice cream. The composition of the product is nothing superfluous: whole milk, cream from cow's milk, sugar cone, butter, water, sugar, filler blueberry, powdered whole milk and skimmed milk, chocolate glaze with vegetable fat, gelling agent - edible gelatin.

  • Ice coffee. The composition of the product is nothing superfluous: scoops of ice cream, brewed coffee, 150 ml water, natural milk, cream, cane sugar and condensed milk. Ice coffee can be served both chilled and still hot, but with the required amount of ice.

  • Milkshakes. An organic milkshake contains twice as much protein as regular milk and most similar milkshakes. It is a natural source of vitamins and minerals. Milkshakes are made from organic milk collected from farms, only natural flavors are used in the production of the cocktail, and they are not too sweet.
